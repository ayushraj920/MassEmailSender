Welcome to Mass Email Sender! Follow these simple steps to send personalized emails effortlessly.

Step 1: Personalize Your Emails

If you want to choose personalization, upload the data through an Excel file. Mass Email Sender will automatically fetch the emails from that Excel file.

Note that if you choose to personalize, then you must have to input emails through the Excel file, and you cannot input them manually.

Step 4: Compose Your Email

Compose your email below. If you chose to personalize earlier, use {{placeholders}} that match with any column in your Excel file. These placeholders will be replaced with the values during sending of the emails.

Step 5: Attach Files

Attach files to your email. Use the "Upload File" button to include documents, PDFs, images, or spreadsheets.

Step 6: Preview Your Email

Before sending your email, click the "Preview Message" button to see how the recipient will view it. Ensure everything looks perfect.
